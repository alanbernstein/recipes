bovik's chai

ingredients (10 quarts)
  • 6 quarts water
  • 4 quarts creamy soy milk - NOT low fat - or cow milk, drink within 2 hours
  • 1 handful coarsely chopped ginger (no need to peel)
  • 1 handful green cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 10 pepper corns
  • a couple cloves
  • 1 cup LEAF black tea - NOT powder
  • 2 cups sucanat - or less cane sugar - or grade b maple syrup
  • optional: cayenne powder
  • optional: cocoa powder
directions
  1. heat water on stove on high
  2. add ginger (coarse chop)
  3. grind up cardamom, cinnamon, peppercorns, cloves, and add to pot
  4. bring water to rolling boil, let boil "until the aroma fills your house"
  5. add sweetener
  6. bring to rolling boil again
  7. add milk
  8. bring to rolling boil again, stirring often and watching
  9. turn off heat and stir in tea, continue stirring until tea is done, 2-3 minutes
  10. pour through a strainer into dispenser
  11. add more sweetener to taste as needed
