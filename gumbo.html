gumbo

ingredients
  • 4 lbs CHICKEN
  • 3 QUARTS OF WATER (sub half water with chicken broth)
  • 1 TEASPOON OF SALT PER QUART OF WATER
  • 2 TEASPOONS OF BLACK PEPPER
  • 4 BUNCHES OF GREEN ONIONS
  • 1 Med white onion
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 4 STALKS OF CELERY
  • 4 LARGE GARLIC PODS
  • 2 JALAPENOS (optional)
  • 1 CUP OF ROUX
  • Season chicken with 2 tablespoons of Tex Joy Steak Seasoning or Fiesta steak seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
preparation
  1. Chop up green onions, celery, garlic and jalapenos.
  2. Boil chicken in water and Tex Joy seasoning.
  3. Add roux and all other ingredients.
  4. Boil for 1 hour. Add salt as desired.
