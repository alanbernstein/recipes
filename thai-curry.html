thai curry

ingredients
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup unsweeted coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp green/yellow curry paste or 3 tbsp red
  • ~1lb chicken breast
  • ~1lb vegetables
  • 1/4 cup sliced basil leaves
  • 1 tsp shredded lime peel (didnt seem to change the flavor much - try the kaffir lime leaves next time?)
  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • basil leaves, lime wedges for garnish
directions
  1. heat oil over medium-high heat in a wok or skillet
  2. add *garlic*, cook for 30 seconds
  3. stir in *coconut milk*, *broth*, *fish sauce*, *brown sugar*
  4. whisk in *curry paste*
  5. bring mixture to boiling
  6. add *chicken*
  7. return to boiling, reduce heat
  8. boil gently uncovered for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  9. stir in *vegetables*
  10. continue to boil gently about 5 more minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink, vegetables are tender, and sauce has thickened some
  11. remove from heat
  12. stir sliced basil leaves and lime peel in, serve over rice with lime wedges and basil leaves
